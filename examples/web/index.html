Pubky Messenger

End-to-end encrypted private messaging over the Pubky network, running entirely in your browser.

This is a demo. Use a throwaway identity. Your recovery phrase or file is processed locally in your browser to derive your keys; it is never uploaded. Do not paste a recovery phrase that protects anything valuable.
